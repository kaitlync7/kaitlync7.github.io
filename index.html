Understand Food Insecurity and Help Combat It

The lack of access to good and healthy food that a person can survive on has only become greater as many people lost jobs over the pandemic and struggle to afford to place food on their plate.

Food Insecurity - A Global Issue

Millions of people are unable to get consistent access to a healthy and sufficient amount of food which can impact their health and well-being. This is known as food insecurity. Food insecurity impacts many communities across the globe and has many causes. My mission is to bring awareness towards this issue so that people experiencing food insecurity will be able to put food on their plate.
